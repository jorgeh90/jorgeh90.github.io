Personal Training

During your Personal Training session you will learn exciting new workout routines, be motivated and inspired, and with full commitment inside and outside the gym, you will build muscle, lose fat, sculpt and shape your physique. I will personally tailor a work out plan for you and will offer nutritional support, coaching, and the motivation you need to achieve change and become a sexier and fitter version of yourself!

Location: I train my clients at Century Sports Club and Day and Night Spa. Free weights, cable machines, resistance bands, medicine balls, Bosu balls, war ropes, TRX, dance studio, pool and cardio equipment make this gym an elite training facility. After your workout you are free to use the spa facilities of Century Sports Club. The Korean spa has several hot tubs, a eucalyptus infused steam room, cold plunge, and multiple saunas that are perfect for your post-workout cool down. In the saunas you can stretch and relax or enjoy one of the therapeutic massages offered by the Century Sports Club. Towel service and lockers are provided upon request, and a full service smoothie bar is available on site.

On-Set: I am excited to provide On-Set location personal training. I offer my services to all network and movie studios. I also offer Role Preparation training and group training sessions for production crews.

Corporate Fitness: I offer a variety of fitness resources and programs designed to bring exercise into your workplace and into the lifestyles of your corporation. A 2010 Harvard Business Review article found that wellness programs, of which fitness is a component, can return as much as six times their cost to the companies that sponsor them. Another 2010 review by a separate team of Harvard researchers, published in the journal Health Affairs, concluded that “medical costs fall by about $3.27 for every dollar spent on wellness programs and that absenteeism costs fall by about $2.73 for every dollar spent.”

Rate: My rate is dependent on my clients needs. I have packages available based on I can best serve you, I will be more then happy to go over my packages with you in person or on the phone. When we talk I will have you tell me about yourself in order to better help me understand your situation. I have a student rate as well, please message me for more info.

Mobile Training: This option is available for those who choose to work at an alternate gym or outside of a gym altogether.﻿ I will meet you at your home, office, apartment or condo gym. I am also available for outdoor workouts in your yard, pool, park, or the beach. Contact me for more information regarding pricing for Mobile Training.

Cancellation Policy: I utilize a 12 hour cancellation policy. Please notify me if you need to cancel a session ASAP or you will be charged for the missed session. Thank you!
