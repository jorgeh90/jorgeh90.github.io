Small Group Personal Training

My small group personal training class is great for those on a budget Whether you're looking to increase size, lose weight, or challenge yourself to get stronger, I will help get you the results you are seeking. This is not a "boot camp" but rather affordable personal training with a few other people sharing the hour with you. Sometimes we will do activities together like stretching and abs or core, but often I will set up each client on a unique work program tailored to their individual needs. "Circuit training" is used as the primary training method. Over the course of an hour, you will use resistance bands, kettle bells, Bosuballs, free weights, cables, and your own body weight for a dynamic, challenging, and fun work out. Classes are offered per class, weekly or discounts are given for pre-paid packages. During the class, you will learn exciting new workout routines, be motivated in a group setting,and with full commitment inside and outside the gym, you will build muscle, lose fat, sculpt and shape your physique. I will offer nutritional support, coaching, and the motivation you need to achieve change and become a sexier and fitter version of yourself! There are 3-6 people in each class.

Location: I train my clients at Century Sports Club and Day and Night Spa.
